# OmniQR: User Instructions (INSTRUCTIONS.md)

A guide for navigating and maximizing the OmniQR Enterprise Console.

## 🧭 Dashboard Navigation

- **Sidebar Toggle**: Switch between **Overview**, **Generator**, **Library**, and **Scan** modules.
- **Sync Status**: ALWAYS check the bottom left. Green (🟢) means your cloud connection is healthy. Red (🔴) means you need to refresh your session.

## 🖼️ hosting a Smart Cloud Asset

1.  Navigate to the **Generator Studio**.
2.  Select **Smart Cloud Asset** (🖼️) from the type dropdown.
3.  Click the **Upload Zone** and select your PDF or Image (PNG/JPG).
4.  Customize your QR styling (colors, shapes, logo).
5.  Click **Generate & Save Asset**.
6.  *Success*: Your file is now hosted in the OmniQR cloud. Scanning the code will instantly open your asset.

## 🔲 Re-Assembling QR Codes

If you have a QR code image that you need to digitalize:
1.  Go to the **Scan Center**.
2.  Drag and drop your QR image or capture it via your camera.
3.  *Magnification*: If the code is small, click the **Zoom 🔍** button to expand it.
4.  *Capture*: Once detected, click **Import to Library** to store the asset digitally.

## 📉 Advanced Features

- **Dynamic Redirects**: Any asset in your library marked "Dynamic" can have its target URL changed at any time without needing to reprint the QR code.
- **Batch Export**: In the Library, you can select multiple codes and click **Export Selected** to download them as a ZIP or a high-resolution PDF sheet.
- **Analytics**: Click the **Stats 📊** icon on any library asset to view the scan history, device types, and time-stamps.

---
**Tip**: Use a unique accent color for different categories (e.g., Red for Social, Blue for Assets) to make your Library easier to scan visually.
